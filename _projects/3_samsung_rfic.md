## Overview

Designed **mass-production RF front-end ICs** for the Samsung Galaxy smartphone series (S7 through S22) at Samsung Electronics System LSI Division (2015–2021). These ICs were fabricated in Samsung's advanced FinFET CMOS processes and shipped in hundreds of millions of units.

### Key Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Products | Galaxy S7, S8, S9, S10, S20, S21, S22 |
| Blocks | LNA, Mixer, TIA |
| Processes | Samsung 14nm / 8nm / 5nm / 4nm FinFET |
| Feature | Multi-band inter/intra-band carrier aggregation |

### Highlights

- **Low-noise amplifier (LNA)** design with industry-leading NF and linearity for cellular bands
- **Wideband receiver** supporting multi-band carrier aggregation (inter + intra-band CA)
- Migrated designs across **four technology nodes** (14nm → 4nm) while maintaining performance
- Co-authored the team's publications at **IEEE RFIC 2017** and **IEEE TMTT 2021**

### Related Publications

- Y. Kim, P. Jang, **T. Jin** et al., "A Current-Efficient Wideband Cellular RF Receiver for Multi-Band Inter- and Intra-Band Carrier Aggregation Using 14nm FinFET CMOS," *IEEE RFIC*, 2017.
- Y. Kim, J. Han, J. Lee, **T. Jin** et al., "Power-Efficient CMOS Cellular RF Receivers for Carrier Aggregation According to RF Front-End Configuration," *IEEE TMTT*, 2021.
