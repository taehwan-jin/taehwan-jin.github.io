## Overview

Designed a **200 Gbps (4 × 50 Gbps) PAM-4 CMOS optical transceiver IC** targeting short-reach data center interconnects. This was a lead designer role at Qualitas Semiconductor (2021–2023).

### Key Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Data Rate | 200 Gbps aggregate (4 × 50 Gbps/lane) |
| Modulation | PAM-4 |
| Process | CMOS |
| Application | Short-reach optical interconnect for data centers |
| Energy Efficiency | 0.85 pJ/bit (TX driver) |

### Highlights

- End-to-end transceiver architecture: **TX driver + RX TIA/LA**
- **VCSEL driver** optimized for linear PAM-4 operation
- Collaborated on VCSEL SPICE modeling for co-simulation with photonic devices
- Results presented at **CLEO-PR 2024**

### Related Publications

- J.-S. Kim, K. Kim, **T. Jin**, P.-S. Han, W. Choi, "4 × 50 Gb/s 0.85 pJ/bit PAM-4 CMOS VCSEL Driver for Linear Pluggable Optics," *CLEO-PR 2024*.
